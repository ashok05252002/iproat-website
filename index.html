CoreHR

Comprehensive Employee Management, document management

Centralizes all employee data and manages employee profiles, document storage, data editing, and secure access controls. Take pro-active and data driven decision through finger tips information on employee’s headcount, diversity demographics by region, age group, gender etc.

Attendance

Designed to streamline time tracking and attendance processes

Create and Configure Customized Time and Attendance Policies Auto penalties based on late come & early go rules. Multiple Shifts Schedule Management with effortless shift charts and Automatic Shift Rotations. Night shift handled flawlessly.

Leave

Unlimited customizable leave types, Leave Carry-Forward, Lapse, Encashment Options

Configures various types of leave (annual leave, sick leave, parental leave, etc.) and associates each with specific policies (e.g., accrual rates, carryover rules). Employees can request leave and Supervisors or HR can approve or deny leave requests based on business rules or availability.

Payroll

Processes salaries based on attendance, leaves, and deductions

Allows businesses to define various pay components and configures the payroll structure to suit various employee categories (e.g., salaried, hourly, part-time, contractors). Supports different tax regimes, exemptions, and deductions. Provides actionable insights into payroll trends and costs

Ess

personal information, payroll details, and HR services through an online portal or mobile application

Employees have real-time access to their information, increasing transparency and reducing the likelihood of errors or misunderstandings. ESS reduces administrative burden for HR by allowing employees to handle routine tasks, such as viewing pay slips and requesting leave.

GeoTagging

Mark attendance easily from anywhere using the mobile app. (Geo-fencing &Geo Tagging)

Provides real-time updates of employee location data, allowing managers to monitor workforce activity and attendance remotely. For employees who need to travel between locations, geo-tagging can track the distance traveled and ensure accurate mileage reimbursements are processed.
